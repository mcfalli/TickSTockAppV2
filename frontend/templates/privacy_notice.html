We use the information we collect to:

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request a copy of your data

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, maintain your session, and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Account data is deleted within 90 days of account closure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy notice from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through our service.
